Total unique post engagements, reach and engagements rates

INSIGHT

 

Total unique post engagements, reach and engagements rates (collectively know as KPIs hereafter) are affected negatively as post frequency decreases.

 

Client currently posts an average of 14 times a week or 2 times a day

 

Historically, many brands found that Facebook hindered the reach of their page’s content the more frequently it posted. In this case, data suggests, that posting more frequently will help the pages KPIs.

RECOMMENDATION

 

Post more frequently. Test posting at various frequencies to determine if there is a decay in engagement, reach and engagement rates related to frequency and if so at what frequency does the decay begin and at what rate do KPIs decay to determine the optimal post frequency.

 

Posting more frequently will likely result in increased engagement, reach, engagement rate and community growth.
